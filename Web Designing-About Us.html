ABOUT US



Welcome To JAV COMMUNICATIONS

We, M/S JAV COMMUNICATIONS LLP., are a Kerala, India based organization that is engaged in Trading and supplying of Mobile accessories such as Travel Charger, Car Charger, Handsfree , Data Cables , Aux Cables , Bluetooth Speakers , Bluetooth , Bluetooth Handsfree and many more with the brand name PORTRONICS,DOTT & MUVTECH We were incorporated in the year 2017 , since then we have offered quality assured products at affordable prices , our product range includes various Mobile accessories and car accessories products . We are one of the fastest growing firms in the mobile accessories segment, with our products being acclaimed across various markets owing to their features such as ease of usage, long functional life ,Elegant and exuisite designed products etc. Ever since our establishment we have been growing steadily, foraying into new markets, with the constant acquisition of new clients.


Our products include a wide range of Mobile Phone Accessories like:
  • Power Banks – It comes in various attractive designs and capacities (from 2000mAh to 16000mAh)
  • Mobile Chargers – Our chargers are made with Smart and Efficient Circuits for fast charging with multiple varieties i.e. wired and USB variants and many output options from 600mA to 8000mA.
  • Car Mobile Chargers – Ranges from a Single USB to Multi USB output with Output Ranging from 1A to 5A.
  • Blue tooth Speakers – Lots of Design options to choose from. Speakers are designed for Engrossing Sound Experience coupled with longer battery Life
  • Mobile Earphones & Headphones – Available in different colours and designs in the Plastic & Metal Forms. All products are made from High quality Material for durability & Exceptional Sound experience.
